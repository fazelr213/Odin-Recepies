Pizza Recipe

This is a delicious pizza that will make your mouth Water

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce

STEPS

  1. Knead and prep the dough
  2. Shred the cheese
  3. Put the dough in a circle shape and spread the cheese and sauce on it
  4. cook it for around 12-15min on medium heat
  5. ENJOYYYYYY
